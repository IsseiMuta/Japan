Japan

Japan is a country located on the east coast of Asia. Japan is known for being a really friendly country with millions tourists visiting Japan all year around. Japan has a population of around 124.5 million.

Japan is also famous for having some delicous traditional foods. One of Japan's most popular, and famous food is sushi.

Japan is made up of 14,125 islands. There are 4 main islands 4 Japan, Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, Kyushu.
